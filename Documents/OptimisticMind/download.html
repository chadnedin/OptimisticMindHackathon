Information

Our app is an easy to use scanner, that scans a patients unique QR code and the displays all the current information of that patient up in the app. Or you can use the webpage by searching the patients name.

How to use the app

In order to use our app , you must apply and be approved as a registered caregiver. Once you have been approved you will receive an email with instructions to complete the registration process. Once registered you can simply login and start using our user friendly app
